This are the Projects that Bill Gates has partaken in

The Windows Operating System (Microsoft)

This is the project that changed the world. By creating a standardized operating system that could run on almost any computer hardware, Gates effectively "democratized" computing. It moved technology out of massive server rooms and onto every desk, turning Microsoft into a global powerhouse and making Gates the world's youngest billionaire at the time.

Polio Eradication (The Gates Foundation)

Through the Global Polio Eradication Initiative, Gates has made it his personal mission to wipe polio off the map. Since the foundation began its work, cases have plummeted by over 99%. It is one of the most ambitious public health projects in history, nearing a status achieved only once before with smallpox: total global extinction of a disease.

The Natrium Nuclear Reactor (TerraPower)

Gates's "moonshot" for the climate crisis is TerraPower. Unlike traditional nuclear plants, the Natrium reactor uses liquid sodium instead of water for cooling, which is safer and more efficient. He is currently building the first-of-its-kind demonstration plant in Wyoming, intending to provide a carbon-free, "always-on" energy source to replace coal plants.
